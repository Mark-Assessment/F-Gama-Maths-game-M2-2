HOW TO PLAY

  1. On click of the StartGame button, Question and random answers will be generated.
  2. Click on one of the box.
  3. If the box clicked is the correct answer, score will be incremented to 1 and alert will be shown displaying a message.
  4. If the answer is wrong you get another chance to choose the correct answer.
  5. There are 60 seconds to play the game. Game will be stopped after 60 seconds.
